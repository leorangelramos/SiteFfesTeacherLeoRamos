Guia Completo de Contrações (Contractions)

Contrações são uma parte fundamental do inglês falado e escrito informal. Elas combinam duas palavras (geralmente um pronome/substantivo e um verbo auxiliar, ou um verbo auxiliar e "not") em uma só, usando um apóstrofo (') para marcar onde as letras foram removidas.

1. Contrações com Verbo "To Be" (am, is, are)

  • I am → I'm (Ex: I'm happy.)
  • You are → You're (Ex: You're late.)
  • He is → He's (Ex: He's a doctor.)
  • She is → She's (Ex: She's smart.)
  • It is → It's (Ex: It's raining.)
  • We are → We're (Ex: We're friends.)
  • They are → They're (Ex: They're coming.)

2. Contrações com "Not" (Negativas)

  • is not → isn't (Ex: He isn't here.)
  • are not → aren't (Ex: They aren't ready.)
  • was not → wasn't (Ex: It wasn't easy.)
  • were not → weren't (Ex: You weren't listening.)
  • do not → don't (Ex: I don't know.)
  • does not → doesn't (Ex: She doesn't like it.)
  • did not → didn't (Ex: We didn't go.)
  • have not → haven't (Ex: I haven't finished.)
  • has not → hasn't (Ex: He hasn't called.)
  • had not → hadn't (Ex: They hadn't seen it.)
  • cannot → can't (Ex: You can't park here.)
  • could not → couldn't (Ex: I couldn't sleep.)
  • should not → shouldn't (Ex: You shouldn't do that.)
  • will not → won't (Ex: I won't be late.)
  • would not → wouldn't (Ex: She wouldn't help.)
  • shall not → shan't (Ex: I shan't be long. - *Mais formal/antigo, comum no Reino Unido*)

3. Contrações com "Will", "Would" e "Had"

  • I will → I'll (Ex: I'll see you tomorrow.)
  • You will → You'll (Ex: You'll be fine.)
  • He / She / It will → He'll / She'll / It'll (Ex: It'll be cold.)
  • We / They will → We'll / They'll (Ex: They'll arrive soon.)
  • I would / I had → I'd (Ex: I'd like some tea. [would] / I'd already left. [had])
  • You would / You had → You'd (Ex: You'd better hurry. [had])

4. Contrações com "Have" e "Has" (Present Perfect)

  • I have → I've (Ex: I've seen that movie.)
  • You have → You've (Ex: You've changed.)
  • He has → He's (Ex: He's gone home. - *Cuidado: igual a "He is"*)
  • She has → She's (Ex: She's finished the test. - *Cuidado: igual a "She is"*)

5. Cuidado: Contrações vs. Palavras Similares (Armadilhas)

Este é o erro mais comum! O apóstrofo nas contrações sempre indica letras faltando, não posse.
